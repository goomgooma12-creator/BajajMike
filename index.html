❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Bajaj Life AWG mein guaranteed income kya hoti hai? ▼
AWG plan mein income 100% guaranteed hai — policy document mein likhi hoti hai. Market se koi lena dena nahi. Example: ₹1L/yr × 10yr PPT → ₹1,81,500/yr guaranteed for 30 years (Second Income variant).
Kya Bajaj Life premiums par GST lagti hai? ▼
Nahi! GOI Notification No. 16/2025 ke anusaar, 22 September 2025 se individual life insurance policies par GST ZERO ho gayi hai.
AWG aur AWG Platinum mein kya fark hai? ▼
AWG mein income PPT ke baad shuru hoti hai. AWG Platinum mein Option 1 (Smart Income) mein PPT ke dauran bhi Early Guaranteed Payouts milti hain. Enhanced 115% ROP bhi available hai.
Claim settlement process kaisi hai? ▼
Bajaj Life ka CSR FY 2024-25 mein 99.29% raha. 96% non-investigative claims 1 working day mein process hote hain — private sector best.
ULIP aur AWG mein kaun better hai? ▼
Risk appetite par depend karta hai. AWG: 100% guaranteed, zero market risk — conservative investors ke liye. ULIP: market-linked, higher potential (8%+) but not guaranteed — long-term wealth (15-20+ years) ke liye. Consult Mike for personalized advice.
Bajaj Life ACE ka Cash Bonus guaranteed hai? ▼
Nahi. ACE mein Guaranteed Income (GI) fixed hai. Cash Bonus company performance par depend karta hai — guaranteed nahi. Yeh participating plan hai.
